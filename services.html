SERVICES

  1. 1. Food and Beverage Services: Taking orders and serving food and drinks with care and attention to detail. Making sure to refill glasses, check in with the guests to ensure their satisfaction.



  2. 2. Table Setting and Clearing: Hosts setting tables with napkins, utensils and glassware and clearing tables once the customers have finished their meals.



  3. 3. Food Preparation: Chefs and kitchen staff prepare dishes according to the customer orders.



  4. 4. Reservation Management: Restaurant managers handling reservations, seating arrangements and accommodating special requests.



  5. 5. Handling Payments: At the end of the meal, the server presents the check to the guests, processes payment and ensures that the guests have a positive dining experience before they leave the restaurant.
